Awesome!

You just installed the Pin It extension!

Instructions

  1. STEP 1: Click on the Pin It icon
  2. STEP 2: Select an element by clicking on it
  3. STEP 3: Drag, resize, or remove selection

Tips

Play around with where you hover to get the perfect selection.

Cancel selecting by clicking the icon again.

If something goes wrong refresh the page.

Caveats

  • It is not recommended to enter data into pinned forms for the reasons listed below.
  • Styling will likely be affected and is expected. Web elements often depend on their context to determine their styling.
  • The interactiveness of some pinned elements may also be affected.
